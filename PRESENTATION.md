## Slide 2: Introduction
**Title:** What is Liosync Sistem?
**Content:**
*   A comprehensive, web-based Point of Sale (POS) system.
*   Designed for **Retail** and **F&B** businesses.
*   **SaaS Architecture:** Built to support multiple companies and branches under one platform.
*   **Goal:** Streamline operations from checkout to back-office financial tracking.

**Speaker Notes:**
*   Introduce Liosync as not just a cash register, but a full business management tool.
*   Highlight the "Multi-Tenant" aspect – it's scalable.

## Slide 3: Key Problems Solved
**Title:** Why Liosync?
**Content:**
*   **Fragmentation:** Unifies Sales, Inventory, and Accounting in one place.
*   **Scalability:** Easily manage 1 or 100 branches with the unique company hierarchy.
*   **Stock Control:** Solves the headache of ingredient tracking with "Bill of Materials" (BOM).
*   **Financial Clarity:** Real-time visibility into Cashflow and Profit & Loss.

**Speaker Notes:**
*   Discuss the common pain points of business owners: unknown stock levels, scattered financial data, and difficulty managing multiple locations.

---

## Slide 4: Core Feature - Point of Sale (POS)
**Title:** Efficient & Flexible POS
**Content:**
*   **Fast Checkout:** Search by name or barcode.
*   **Pending Orders:** Hold tables or carts and resume later.
*   **Receipts:** Thermal printing support for Cashier, Kitchen, and Tables.
*   **Payment Options:** Cash, QRIS, and dynamic Tax calculations.

**Speaker Notes:**
*   Emphasize speed and reliability at the counter.
*   Mention the specific support for F&B workflows (kitchen receipts, holding orders).

---

## Slide 5: Core Feature - Inventory Management
**Title:** Advanced Inventory Tracking
**Content:**
*   **Unified Items:** Manage Products (Sellable) and Raw Materials (Consumable).
*   **Smart Recipes (BOM):** Selling a "Burger" automatically deducts "Bun," "Patty," and "Cheese."
*   **Stock Transactions:** Full audit trail of every modification.
*   **Restocking:** Purchase Order workflow with cost tracking.

**Speaker Notes:**
*   This is a key differentiator. Many simple POS systems track sold items, but Liosync tracks *ingredients* via recipes.

---

## Slide 6: Core Feature - Financials & Reports
**Title:** Know Your Numbers
**Content:**
*   **Cashflow:** Track petty cash, expenses, and automated sales income.
*   **Profit & Loss:** Revenue minus Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) analysis.
*   **Detailed Reports:** Sales logs, Inventory valuation, and Export options (PDF/Excel).

**Speaker Notes:**
*   Business owners need to know if they are profitable, not just how much they sold.

---

## Slide 7: The Multi-Tenant Model
**Title:** Built for Growth (SaaS)
**Content:**
*   **Superadmin:** Manages the platform, subscriptions, and global settings.
*   **Company Admin:** The business owner managing their brand and users.
*   **Branch Admin:** Managers focused on store-level operations.
*   **Subscriptions:** Manual bank transfer flow with Admin verification for enabling features.

**Speaker Notes:**
*   Explain the hierarchy. This structure is secure and keeps data isolated between different client companies.
